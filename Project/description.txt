The Ketogenic Diet is a great way to loose weight without starving yourself. 
The Ketogenic Diet utilizes dietary fats to form ketone bodies for fuel. 
The Ketogenic Diet is 70% of your caloric intake should be fats, 20% should be 
protein and 10% should be carbohydrates. This website gives a general overview of the diet, 
describes dietary fats, protein, carbohydrates, and gives sample recipes. You can select 
different types of foods to have your own recipe suggestions sent back to you.
